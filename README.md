# ☕ The Coffee Bean — Logo Redesign Project

## 🎯 Task Overview
The objective of this project is to redesign the existing logo identity for The Coffee Bean (Est. 2026) to explore fresh, modern visual directions while maintaining core brand recognition.

---

## 🎨 Design Variations & Professional Logic

### 🏛️ Variation 01: The Editorial Minimalist Concept
![Variation 1](./CodeAlpha%20variant%201%20THE%20COFFEE%20BEAN.png)

This concept establishes a highly legible, identity-first framework tailored for high-frequency corporate assets. By replacing background distractions with an organic, low-fatigue cream palette, the design achieves maximum visual contrast and complies with modern accessibility standards. The typographic architecture employs a heavy, geometric sans-serif for 'THE COFFEE BEAN' to ground the intricate central iconography, while the tracked serif for 'EST. 2026' introduces an editorial undertone.

---

### 🌟 Variation 02: The Premium Artisanal Concept
![Variation 2](./CodeAlpha%20variant%202png.png)

This iteration explores premium brand positioning, shifting the identity from an everyday establishment to a luxury specialty roastery. The background leverages a sophisticated, calculated ambient light gradient that mimics upscale cafe interiors and creates an atmospheric depth of field. The primary typography transitions to a high-contrast traditional serif, treated with a structural metallic gradient that mirrors professional brass finishes.

---

### 🌙 Variation 03: The Digital-First Contemporary Concept
![Variation 3](./CodeAlha%20varinat%203THE%20COFFEE%20BEAN%20(1).jpg)

Designed with a digital-first lifestyle focus, this variation targets digital natives and mobile-centric consumers. The asset features a technical 'Dark Mode' canvas that minimizes screen glare and allows the central element to pop aggressively. Breaking traditional coffee branding conventions, the typography introduces a vibrant, progressive color gradient that shifts seamlessly across warm color spectrums.
